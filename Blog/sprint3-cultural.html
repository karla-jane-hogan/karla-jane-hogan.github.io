Emotional Intelligence

What is Emotional Intelligence?

Its a collection of skills - the ability to be aware of ones own feelings and emotions and other people emotions and how you think and act on those emotions.

How is it different to IQ?

EI is about the emotions and how you feel, think about those emotions and act

Why is it important?

It is important to learn the EI skills to be the best version operate in an optimal state. Your mind, body and soul to be in alignment, which can cultivate a clear, calm mind to deal with everyday life. Three benefits for learning EI skills are; Stellar performance, Outstanding Leadership, and Happiness.
